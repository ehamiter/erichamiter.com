Chill Destruction

Ever want to just chill and launch projectiles into things to make them explode?

Well, your time has come.

Relax to some ambient beats while you fling your hapless space vehicle into gargantuan-sized objects in space to watch them explode into ever-smaller pieces, until there is no more. Once all objects have been destroyed, a new round generates with new shapes.

So, chill... and destroy.
